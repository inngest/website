Event-driven systems rely on event matching and filtering. At scale, optimizing the efficiency of these systems usually point to separation with topics or applying filters at the consumer level. This all works well when your rules are known and defined up front, but what about if they aren’t able to be defined up front? What about if they’re short lived for seconds, minutes or days?
